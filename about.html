Professional Background

I currently work on the ASDR (Analytical Solution Data Refresh) team at Accenture, where I focus entirely on backend data operations — designing, maintaining, and supporting scalable ETL pipelines that extract, transform, and load data from multiple cloud-based sources into internal systems used for reporting and decision-making.

We use platforms like Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Microsoft Azure, and I manage the full data refresh lifecycle — including automation, orchestration, scheduling, and validation of data loads across clients. Even though the final data feeds dashboards like Power BI, I don’t work on visualization; I own the backend workflows that make sure the data is always accurate, complete, and timely.

I'm known as the team’s "happy pill"—positive, dependable, and always ready to help.
